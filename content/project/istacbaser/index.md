## ISTAC

The Canary Islands Statistics Institute \([Instituto Canario de Estad√≠tica, ISTAC](http://www.gobiernodecanarias.org/istac/)\) is the central organ of the autonomous statistical system and official research center of the Government of the Canary Islands, created and regulated by Law 1/1991 of 28 January, Statistics of the Autonomous Community of the Canary Islands.

## istacbaser

The istacbaser R-package allows researchers to quickly search and download the data of their particular interest in a programmatic and reproducible fashion; this facilitates a seamless integration into their workflow and allows analysis to be quickly rerun on different areas of interest and with realtime access to the latest available data.
